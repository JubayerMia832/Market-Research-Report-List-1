<p><p>The mobile water treatment systems market encompasses various technologies tailored for effective water purification. Microfiltration uses porous membranes to remove larger particles and bacteria, while ultrafiltration further eliminates smaller contaminants, including some viruses. Nano-filtration bridges the gap, targeting divalent ions and organic molecules. Reverse osmosis enhances this process by pushing water through a semipermeable membrane, achieving high purity levels. Disinfection methods, such as UV treatment or chlorination, eliminate pathogens, ensuring safe drinking water for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
