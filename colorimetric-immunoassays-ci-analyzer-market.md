<p><p>Colorimetric immunoassays (CI) analyzers come in two primary types: open-ended systems and closed-ended systems. Open-ended systems allow flexibility in assay development, enabling users to customize protocols and reagents as needed. This adaptability is beneficial for research applications. Conversely, closed-ended systems are pre-configured with specific assays and reagents, offering convenience and standardization, ideal for clinical settings. The choice between these systems largely depends on the user's requirements for flexibility versus ease of use and consistency in results.</p></p>
