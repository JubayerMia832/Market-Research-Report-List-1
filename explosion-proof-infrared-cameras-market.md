<p><p>The explosion-proof infrared camera market is categorized into three types based on infrared wavelength: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave infrared cameras operate in the 0.9 to 2.5 micrometer range, suitable for detecting water and other materials. Medium wave cameras, from 3 to 5 micrometers, are effective for thermal imaging and industrial applications. Long wave infrared cameras, spanning 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in low-light conditions, making them ideal for nighttime surveillance and monitoring hazardous environments.</p></p>
