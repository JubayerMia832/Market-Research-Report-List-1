<p><p>The osteoarthritis therapeutics market consists of three primary types: oral, injection, and external. Oral therapies include nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) and analgesics, providing convenient management of symptoms. Injection treatments, such as corticosteroids and hyaluronic acid, target affected joints directly for more effective relief. External therapies involve topical medications like creams and patches applied to the skin, offering localized pain relief with minimal systemic effects. Each type plays a crucial role in managing osteoarthritis symptoms and enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
