<p><p>Inline samplers are critical devices used in various industries for collecting representative samples of bulk materials. The market can be divided into two main types: manual and pneumatic. Manual inline samplers require physical operation for sample collection, offering simplicity and lower costs, but may be less efficient for large volumes. Pneumatic inline samplers, on the other hand, use compressed air or vacuum systems to automate sample collection, providing higher efficiency and consistency, making them ideal for high-throughput applications.</p></p>
