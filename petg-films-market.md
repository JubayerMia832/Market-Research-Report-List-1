<p><p>PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) films are a type of thermoplastic polyester film known for their clarity, durability, chemical resistance, and ease of thermoforming. PETG films are preferred for their ability to maintain transparency, withstand high temperatures, and provide a high-quality finish, making them ideal for applications like food packaging, labels, and protective coverings.</p><p>The PETG Films Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly packaging solutions. <p><p>PETG films are categorized based on shrinkage rates: **Shrinkage <60%** films are ideal for applications requiring minimal dimensional change, such as durable labels and protective layers. **Shrinkage 60-70%** films balance flexibility and formability, commonly used in packaging and heat-shrink sleeves. **Shrinkage >70%** films offer high contraction, perfect for tight-fitting applications like tamper-evident seals and bottle sleeves. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal performance in terms of durability, fit, and functionality across various sectors.</p></p>
