<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) cooler market primarily includes two main types: plate wing and shell and tube coolers. </p><p>Plate wing coolers utilize a series of thin plates to transfer heat efficiently, offering a compact design and improved thermal performance. In contrast, shell and tube coolers consist of a series of tubes housed within a shell, facilitating heat exchange through separate fluid paths. Both types are essential for reducing emissions in automotive and industrial applications by regulating EGR temperature and enhancing engine efficiency.</p></p>
