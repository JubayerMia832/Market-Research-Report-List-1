<p><p>The resin diluent and modifier market is categorized into non-reactive and reactive types. Non-reactive diluents are solvents that blend with resins to adjust viscosity without chemical interaction, maintaining stability and performance. In contrast, reactive diluents chemically bond with resin components, enhancing properties like flexibility or curing speed. These distinctions are crucial for industries such as coatings, adhesives, and composites, where specific performance criteria are required in applications ranging from automotive to construction. Both types play vital roles in optimizing resin formulations.</p></p>
