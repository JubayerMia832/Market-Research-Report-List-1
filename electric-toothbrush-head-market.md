<p><strong>The Electric Toothbrush Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Linear</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric toothbrush head market can be categorized into two primary types: reciprocating linear and rotary motion. Reciprocating linear toothbrush heads move back and forth, effectively mimicking the motion of traditional brushing, allowing for thorough plaque removal. In contrast, rotary motion heads rotate in a circular motion, often providing a wider reach and enhance cleaning efficiency, especially between teeth. Both designs cater to diverse consumer preferences and oral care needs, resulting in a competitive market landscape.</p></p>
