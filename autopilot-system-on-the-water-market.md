<p><p>The autopilot system on the water market encompasses three main types: mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic systems. Mechanical autopilot systems utilize physical components, such as cables and rods, to control steering, providing reliability but limited automation. Electrical systems use electronic components for precise control, offering enhanced automation and integration with navigation systems. Hydraulic autopilots employ fluid power to drive steering mechanisms, offering strong performance and responsiveness, ideal for larger vessels. Each type caters to specific operational needs and vessel sizes.</p></p>
