<p><p>Vacuum sputter equipment market is segmented into several types, including DC sputtering, RF sputtering, and others. DC sputtering utilizes direct current to deposit thin films, primarily on conductive substrates, making it suitable for metals. RF sputtering employs radio frequency energy, allowing deposition on insulating materials due to its capability to generate plasma. Other methods may include magnetron sputtering and pulsed laser deposition, each varying in technique and application, catering to diverse industries such as electronics, optics, and solar cells.</p></p>
