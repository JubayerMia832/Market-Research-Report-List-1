Butyl rubber tape is highly flexible and resistant to UV light, suitable for various roofing applications. Liquid silicone tape offers excellent durability and weather resistance, forming a strong seal on different substrates. Each type serves specific needs in roof repairs, ensuring longevity and protection from leaks.</p></p>
