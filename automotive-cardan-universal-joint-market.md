<p><p>The Automotive Cardan Universal Joint market is divided into three main types: Single Cardan, Double Cardan, and Others. Single Cardan joints consist of two yokes connected by a cross shaft, suitable for moderate angular misalignments. Double Cardan joints feature two yokes interconnected, allowing for greater angular flexibility and used in more demanding applications. "Others" encompasses specialized joints like telescopic and constant velocity types, catering to specific vehicle requirements and enhancing drive efficiency across varying terrain.</p></p>
