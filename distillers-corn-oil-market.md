<p><strong>The Distillers Corn Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Milling</li><li>Wet-Milling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distillers corn oil market can be classified into two main types: dry-milling and wet-milling. In dry-milling, corn is processed to produce ethanol and animal feed, leading to the extraction of corn oil as a byproduct. This method is more efficient and cost-effective. Conversely, wet-milling involves soaking corn in water to extract starch, gluten, and oil, producing higher-quality oil but at a greater cost. Both methods cater to different industrial needs, impacting oil quality and market dynamics.</p></p>
