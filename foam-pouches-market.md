<p><ul><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE)</li><li>Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam pouches market can be segmented into four primary types based on the density of polyethylene: High Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE), Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE offers superior strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. MDPE provides a balance between flexibility and resistance. LDPE is known for its softness and flexibility, suitable for lightweight packaging. LLDPE combines properties of HDPE and LDPE, offering enhanced stretchability and puncture resistance. Each type serves specific packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
