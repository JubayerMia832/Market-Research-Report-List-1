<p><p>The hemp seed extract market consists of two primary types: liquid extract and powder extract. Liquid extract is derived by infusing hemp seeds in a solvent, offering concentrated nutritional benefits and easy incorporation into beverages and tinctures. In contrast, powder extract involves grinding hemp seeds into a fine powder, preserving their nutrients for use in food products, supplements, and cosmetics. Both forms cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications, driving growth in health, wellness, and dietary supplement sectors.</p></p>
