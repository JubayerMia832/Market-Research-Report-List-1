<p><strong>The Twin Screw Extruders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Co-rotating TSE</li><li>Parallel Counter-rotating TSE</li><li>Conical Twin Screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin screw extruder (TSE) market comprises three main types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical twin screw extruders. Parallel co-rotating TSEs feature two screws that rotate in the same direction, enhancing mixing and conveying efficiency. In contrast, parallel counter-rotating TSEs have screws rotating in opposite directions, providing better shear and control over material processing. Conical twin screw extruders, with a tapered design, offer advantages in processing various materials and are often used in applications requiring gentle handling or melting.</p></p>
