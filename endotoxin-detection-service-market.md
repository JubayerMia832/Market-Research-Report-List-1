<p><strong>The Endotoxin Detection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Clot Endotoxin Test</li><li>Chromogenic Endotoxin Test</li><li>Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endotoxin detection service market comprises three main testing types. The Gel Clot Endotoxin Test utilizes gel formation to indicate endotoxin presence, offering a simple and cost-effective method. The Chromogenic Endotoxin Test employs chromogenic substrates that produce a color change, providing quantitative results with high sensitivity. The Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test measures changes in turbidity caused by endotoxins, allowing for rapid quantification. Each method caters to specific industry needs, ensuring safety in pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and biotechnology.</p></p>
