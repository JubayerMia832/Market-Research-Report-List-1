<p><p>The compression spring market features various types, each designed for specific applications. Conical springs taper from a wider diameter to a narrower one, enhancing stability and minimizing sideways movement. Hourglass springs have a distinctly hourglass shape, allowing for better space utilization and compressive resistance. Barrel-shaped springs maintain a uniform spiral, offering consistent load distribution and strength. Each type serves unique mechanical needs, with varying characteristics that cater to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
