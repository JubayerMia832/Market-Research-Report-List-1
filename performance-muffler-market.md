<p><strong>The Performance Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The performance muffler market can be categorized into two main types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers reduce sound by absorbing sound waves through porous materials, effectively minimizing noise while maintaining exhaust flow. Reactive mufflers, on the other hand, use acoustic chambers and resonators to create sound cancellation through wave interference, resulting in a more refined exhaust note. Both types aim to enhance vehicle performance and sound quality but employ different mechanisms to achieve their respective goals.</p></p>
