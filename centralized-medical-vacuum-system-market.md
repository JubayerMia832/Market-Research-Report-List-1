<p><p>The Centralized Medical Vacuum System market includes various technologies, such as Dry Claw Pump Technology, Dry Rotary Vane Technology, and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology. Dry Claw Pumps operate without oil, reducing contamination risk, while Dry Rotary Vane Technology utilizes vanes that are not lubricated, offering efficiency and lower maintenance. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology uses oil for sealing and cooling, providing high performance but requiring regular maintenance. Each type serves specific needs within healthcare environments, influencing market demand and applications.</p></p>
