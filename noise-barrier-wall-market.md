<p><p>The noise barrier wall market comprises two primary types: ground-mounted and structure-mounted noise barriers. Ground-mounted noise barriers are installed directly on the ground, often alongside highways or railways, effectively reducing noise pollution in neighboring areas. In contrast, structure-mounted noise barriers are affixed to existing structures like buildings or bridges, serving to diminish noise transmission from traffic or industrial activities. Both types aim to enhance environmental quality and improve the acoustic comfort of residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
