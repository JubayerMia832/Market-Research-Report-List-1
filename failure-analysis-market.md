<p><ul><li>Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)</li><li>Focused Ion Beam (FIB) System</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM)</li><li>Dual Beam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The failure analysis market encompasses advanced imaging technologies essential for understanding material and component failures. Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) provide high-resolution surface imaging, revealing microstructural defects. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) systems enable precise sample preparation and modification, facilitating detailed diagnostics. Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) offer insights into internal structures at the atomic level, essential for assessing material integrity. Dual Beam Systems combine SEM and FIB capabilities, enhancing flexibility and depth of analysis for comprehensive failure investigations in various industries.</p></p>
