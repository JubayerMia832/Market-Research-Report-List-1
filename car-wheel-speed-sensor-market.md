<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two primary types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use the Hall effect to detect wheel speed, offering high accuracy and reliability, especially in harsh conditions. They are commonly used in modern vehicles for ABS and traction control systems. Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on magnetic fields and induction to measure speed, known for their durability and cost-effectiveness. Both types are essential for vehicle safety and performance, catering to different automotive needs.</p></p>
