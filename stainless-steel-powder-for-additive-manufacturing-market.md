<p><p>The stainless steel powder market for additive manufacturing includes Austenitic, Martensitic, and Ferritic grades. Austenitic grades, like 316L, are corrosion-resistant, non-magnetic, and widely used in aerospace and medical applications. Martensitic grades, such as 17-4PH, offer high strength and hardness, ideal for tools and automotive parts. Ferritic grades, like 430, are magnetic, cost-effective, and used in automotive and industrial applications due to their moderate corrosion resistance. Each grade caters to specific performance needs, driving their adoption in diverse industries.</p></p>
