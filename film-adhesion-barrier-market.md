<p><strong>The Film Adhesion Barrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non-absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Film Adhesion Barrier Market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable barriers are designed to dissolve over time, ideally reducing the risk of adhesions while promoting healing without the need for a second procedure to remove them. Non-absorbable barriers, on the other hand, remain in the body permanently, providing long-term protection against adhesions but may require surgical intervention for removal. Both types play vital roles in surgical settings, helping to enhance patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
