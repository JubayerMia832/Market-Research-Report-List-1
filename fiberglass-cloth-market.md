<p><p>The fiberglass cloth market comprises three main types: high alkali cloth, alkali cloth, and alkali-free cloth. </p><p>High alkali cloth contains a higher percentage of alkaline materials, making it suitable for applications requiring greater chemical resistance but less mechanical strength. Alkali cloth has moderate alkaline content, providing a balance between cost and performance, ideal for general-purpose use. Alkali-free cloth, on the other hand, is designed for enhanced durability and strength, making it suitable for demanding applications like aerospace and automotive industries, where high performance is crucial.</p></p>
