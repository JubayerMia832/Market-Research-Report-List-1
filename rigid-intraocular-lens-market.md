<p><p>The rigid intraocular lens market includes various types designed to enhance vision post-cataract surgery. Multifocal intraocular lenses allow for clear vision at multiple distances, reducing the need for glasses. Adjustable intraocular lenses enable surgeons to modify the lens power after implantation for optimal vision correction. Aspheric intraocular lenses are crafted to reduce spherical aberration, providing sharper vision, particularly in low-light conditions. Each type serves distinct patient needs, fostering advancements in cataract treatment and improving overall visual outcomes.</p></p>
