Metarhizium anisopliae also functions as a fungus, infecting and killing various insect species. These bio-insecticides are eco-friendly alternatives to chemical pesticides, promoting sustainable agricultural practices and reducing environmental impact. Their popularity is growing due to increasing demand for organic farming solutions.</p></p>
