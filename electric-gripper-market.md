<p><p>The electric gripper market is primarily segmented into two types based on the number of fingers: Two-Finger Electric Grippers and Three-Finger Electric Grippers. Two-finger grippers are designed for simple, precise handling of objects with parallel sides, making them ideal for pick-and-place applications. In contrast, three-finger grippers provide more stability and versatility, allowing for the handling of various shapes and sizes. This flexibility expands their utility across industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and assembly, enhancing automation processes.</p></p>
