<p><strong>The CO2 Gas Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Siphon Gas Tank</li><li>Anti-siphon Gas Tank</li><li>Ordinary Gas Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CO2 gas tank market comprises three primary types: siphon gas tanks, anti-siphon gas tanks, and ordinary gas tanks. Siphon gas tanks allow liquid CO2 to be drawn from the bottom, making them ideal for applications requiring high flow rates. Anti-siphon gas tanks feature mechanisms that prevent liquid CO2 transfer, ensuring gas-only output, suitable for sensitive equipment. Ordinary gas tanks store gaseous CO2 and are commonly used where direct gas delivery is needed. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
