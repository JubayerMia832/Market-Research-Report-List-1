<p><strong>The Mold Temperature Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Mold Temperature Controller</li><li>Oil Mold Temperature Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mold Temperature Controller market is primarily divided into two types: water and oil mold temperature controllers. Water mold temperature controllers utilize water as a cooling medium, offering efficient temperature regulation for processes requiring precise heat control, often preferred for their cost-effectiveness and lower maintenance. In contrast, oil mold temperature controllers use oil, providing higher thermal stability and superior heat retention, ideal for processes needing elevated temperatures. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing mold performance and product quality across various industries.</p></p>
