<p><strong>The Fire Protection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Fire Protection Systems</li><li>Passive Fire Protection Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire protection systems are categorized into two main types: active and passive systems. Active fire protection systems involve measures that require intervention to control fire, such as sprinklers, alarms, and fire extinguishers, which activate during a fire event. In contrast, passive fire protection systems aim to contain or slow the spread of fire without active intervention, including fire-resistant walls, doors, and structural elements. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing safety and minimizing fire damage in various environments.</p></p>
