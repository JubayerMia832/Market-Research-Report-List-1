<p><p>The Natural Fiber Polymer Composites market includes three primary types: Non-Woven, Woven, and Specialty Forms. Non-Woven composites involve fibers bonded together without weaving, offering flexibility and lightweight properties, often used in automotive and packaging industries. Woven composites are made by interlacing fibers, providing strength and durability, suitable for structural applications. Specialty Forms encompass unique configurations like mats or panels, catering to specific applications in construction, textiles, and consumer goods, emphasizing sustainability and performance in various sectors.</p></p>
