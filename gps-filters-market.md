<p><strong>The GPS Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW Filters</li><li>BAW Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS filters market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters utilize surface acoustic waves for signal processing and are typically used in applications requiring compact size and lower frequency ranges. In contrast, BAW filters leverage bulk acoustic waves, providing better performance at higher frequencies and improved selectivity. Both types are essential in enhancing GPS signal clarity and reliability, catering to various consumer electronics and communication devices.</p></p>
