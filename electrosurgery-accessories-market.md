<p><p>The electrosurgery accessories market encompasses various types, including generators and surgical instruments categorized into bipolar and monopolar systems. Generators provide the necessary power for surgical procedures. Bipolar instruments use two electrodes for tissue dissection and coagulation, offering precision and reduced thermal spread. Monopolar instruments utilize a single electrode, allowing for versatile cutting and coagulation. Additionally, argon ion systems enhance coagulation through argon gas delivery, while smoke management systems ensure a safe surgical environment by removing smoke and particulates generated during procedures.</p></p>
