<p><strong>The Bone Meal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Boiled Bone Meal</li><li>Rough Bone Meal</li><li>Steamed Bone Meal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone meal market is categorized into three main types: boiled bone meal, rough bone meal, and steamed bone meal. Boiled bone meal is processed through boiling, which enhances nutrient availability, making it ideal for organic fertilizers. Rough bone meal is less processed, retaining larger particles and is often used for livestock feed. Steamed bone meal involves steaming bones under pressure, minimizing pathogens while preserving nutrients for both agricultural and animal feed applications. Each type serves distinct agricultural and dietary purposes.</p></p>
