<p><strong>The Table Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halite</li><li>Sea Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The table salt market primarily consists of two main types: halite and sea salt. Halite, commonly mined from salt deposits, is often refined and contains additives to prevent clumping. It is economical and widely used in cooking and food processing. Sea salt, harvested from evaporated seawater, retains trace minerals and offers a distinct flavor, making it popular in gourmet cooking. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary applications, shaping a vibrant and competitive market landscape.</p></p>
