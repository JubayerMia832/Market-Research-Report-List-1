<p><p>The fuel antistat market is divided into two primary types: metal and non-metallic antistatic agents. Metal antistatic agents include compounds like copper and zinc, which help reduce static electricity in fuels, enhancing safety and performance. Non-metallic antistatic agents encompass organic compounds and additives that serve a similar purpose without metals, often providing additional benefits such as improved fuel stability. Both types play a crucial role in preventing static discharge during fuel handling and transportation, ensuring operational safety.</p></p>
