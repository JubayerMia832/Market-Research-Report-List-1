<p><p>The Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) Assay Kit market primarily encompasses two types: the Chemiluminescence Method and the Microparticle Enzyme-Linked Immunoassay (MEIA). The Chemiluminescence Method relies on light emission for detecting CEA levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results. In contrast, MEIA utilizes microparticles coated with specific antibodies, allowing for simultaneous detection of multiple analytes with enhanced accuracy. Both methods are essential for diagnosing and monitoring certain cancers, driving demand in clinical laboratories and healthcare settings.</p></p>
