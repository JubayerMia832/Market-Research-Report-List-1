<p><strong>The Lighting Contactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Held</li><li>Mechanically Held</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting contactors market comprises two main types: electrically held and mechanically held. Electrically held contactors use an electromagnetic coil to maintain closure, providing quick and efficient operation, often used in automated lighting systems. Conversely, mechanically held contactors utilize a latching mechanism, retaining their position without continuous power, which enhances energy efficiency and reliability. Both types serve critical roles in controlling lighting systems in commercial and industrial applications, addressing diverse needs for performance and power management.</p></p>
