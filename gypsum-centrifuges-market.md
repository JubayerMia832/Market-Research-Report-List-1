<p><p>The gypsum centrifuge market comprises two main types: horizontal and vertical centrifuges. Horizontal centrifuges operate with a rotating drum that spins in a horizontal plane, efficiently separating materials based on density. They are commonly used for large-scale gypsum processing. Vertical centrifuges, on the other hand, utilize a vertical orientation, allowing for better liquid drainage and concentration of solids. Each type is suited for specific applications, catering to the diverse needs of the gypsum industry in processing and separating materials effectively.</p></p>
