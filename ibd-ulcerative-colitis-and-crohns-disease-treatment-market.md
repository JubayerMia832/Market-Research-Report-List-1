<p><p>The IBD treatment market comprises various drug types targeting Ulcerative Colitis and Crohn's Disease. TNF inhibitors, such as infliximab and adalimumab, reduce inflammation by blocking tumor necrosis factor. Aminosalicylates, like mesalamine, target the intestines to relieve symptoms. Integrin antagonists, such as vedolizumab, prevent white blood cells from migrating to inflamed gut tissues. Corticosteroids, including prednisone, swiftly reduce inflammation but are used for short-term management due to potential side effects. Each category plays a crucial role in managing these chronic conditions.</p></p>
