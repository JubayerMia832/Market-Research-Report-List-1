<p><p>Confocal Laser Scanning Microscopes (CLSM) are advanced imaging systems used to obtain high-resolution, three-dimensional images of specimens. The market is primarily divided into two types: single-photon CLSM and multiphoton CLSM. Single-photon CLSM utilizes a single laser excitation source for imaging, ideal for routine applications. In contrast, multiphoton CLSM employs two or more photons for excitation, enabling deeper tissue imaging with reduced phototoxicity, making it suitable for live-cell studies and complex biological samples.</p></p>
