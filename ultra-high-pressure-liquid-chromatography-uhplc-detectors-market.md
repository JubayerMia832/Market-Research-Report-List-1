<p><p>The Ultra High Pressure Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) detectors market includes several types catering to diverse analytical needs. UV-Visible detectors are commonly used for detecting compounds with chromophores, offering excellent sensitivity and versatility. Refractive Index Detectors measure changes in the refractive index of a mobile phase, ideal for non-UV active compounds. Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD) provide detection for a wide range of analytes, including those lacking UV absorbance. Fluorescence detectors offer high sensitivity for specific compounds. Other detectors include mass spectrometry and conductivity detectors, expanding application possibilities in various industries.</p></p>
