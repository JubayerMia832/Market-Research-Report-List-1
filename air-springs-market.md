<p><p>The air springs market is categorized into three main types: convoluted, sleeves, and others. Convoluted air springs feature a flexible design with multiple convolutions, providing better load support and comfort, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Sleeve air springs have a cylindrical shape, offering a simpler structure for lighter loads, often seen in commercial vehicles. Other types include various specialized designs tailored for specific industrial applications, ensuring versatility across different sectors like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
