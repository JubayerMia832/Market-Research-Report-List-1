<p><p>The Industrial Grade Optical Network Unit (ONU) market comprises two primary types: Active Optical Network Units (AONUs) and Passive Optical Network Units (PONUs). AONUs utilize active components to receive, process, and transmit data, providing high flexibility and performance, making them suitable for dynamic industrial environments. Conversely, PONUs rely on passive components, offering lower operational costs and energy efficiency by splitting signals without needing active equipment, ideal for stable and often less demanding applications. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing industrial connectivity.</p></p>
