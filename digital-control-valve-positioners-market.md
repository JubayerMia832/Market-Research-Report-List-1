<p><p>Digital control valve positioners are essential devices in automation, enhancing valve performance and responsiveness. The market is segmented into two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners use a spring to return the valve to a fail-safe position when there’s a loss of signal, making them ideal for specific applications. In contrast, double acting positioners provide equal force for both opening and closing, offering greater control and precision in demanding processes. This differentiation caters to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
