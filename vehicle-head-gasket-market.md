<p><p>The vehicle head gasket market is classified into three main types: non-metallic, semi-metallic, and metallic. Non-metallic gaskets, typically made from compressed fiber or rubber, offer good sealing but may face durability challenges. Semi-metallic gaskets combine materials like steel and elastomers, providing enhanced strength and thermal resistance, making them suitable for high-performance engines. Metallic gaskets, crafted from materials such as aluminum or copper, deliver superior longevity and sealing under extreme conditions, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific engine requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
