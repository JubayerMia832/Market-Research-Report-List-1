<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test kits market includes various types such as immunoturbidimetric, ELISA, CLIA, and others. Immunoturbidimetric tests measure the cloudiness in a sample due to CRP-antibody complexes, providing rapid results. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a sensitive method that quantifies CRP levels using enzyme-linked antibodies. CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay) employs luminescence for highly sensitive detection. Other market types may include point-of-care tests or rapid strip tests, catering to diverse diagnostic needs and settings.</p></p>
