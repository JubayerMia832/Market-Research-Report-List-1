<p><ul><li>High Flow Insufflator</li><li>Medium Flow Insufflator</li><li>Low Flow Insufflator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insufflator market is categorized into high, medium, and low flow insufflators based on their gas flow rates. High flow insufflators deliver a rapid flow of gas, ideal for large volume procedures requiring quick inflation, while medium flow insufflators provide a balanced flow suitable for standard surgical applications. Low flow insufflators, on the other hand, focus on precise inflation at controlled rates, making them particularly useful for delicate or minimal invasiveness surgeries. Each type serves specific procedural needs in medical settings.</p></p>
