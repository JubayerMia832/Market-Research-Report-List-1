<p><p>The polyurethane timing belt market features two primary types: trapezoidal tooth and arc tooth timing belts. Trapezoidal tooth polyurethane timing belts are characterized by their angular teeth profile, providing efficient engagement and minimizing backlash, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, arc tooth polyurethane timing belts have a rounded tooth profile that enhances flexibility and reduces noise, leading to smoother operation. Both types are favored in various industries for their durability, performance, and ability to withstand harsh conditions.</p></p>
