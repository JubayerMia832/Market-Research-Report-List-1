<p><p>The Intelligent All-Wheel Drive (AWD) System market includes three primary types based on default drivetrain configurations. **Front Wheel Drive by Default** prioritizes front-wheel power for efficiency, engaging rear wheels only when needed for traction. **Four Wheel Drive by Default** distributes power evenly across all wheels, ideal for off-road or rugged conditions. **Rear Wheel Drive by Default** emphasizes rear-wheel performance, engaging front wheels for stability during acceleration or slippery conditions. Each type optimizes performance, efficiency, and traction based on driving needs.</p></p>
