<p><strong>The Flexible AMOLED Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible AMOLED panels market encompasses two main types: Active-Matrix OLED (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays use an active matrix of controls to enhance color accuracy and refresh rates, making them ideal for smartphones and high-end devices. In contrast, PMOLED displays utilize a simpler passive matrix, leading to lower cost and power consumption but limited resolution and size flexibility. These technologies serve varied applications, from consumer electronics to wearables, reflecting diverse market demands.</p></p>
