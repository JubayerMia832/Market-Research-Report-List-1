<p><p>The High Water Alarm market is categorized into AC Powered and DC Powered systems. AC Powered alarms typically use alternating current to operate, making them suitable for home or commercial setups where a stable electricity supply is available. In contrast, DC Powered alarms rely on direct current, often powered by batteries, providing flexibility for remote locations or backup scenarios. Both types serve the same purpose of alerting users to rising water levels, but their power sources determine their application and reliability in various environments.</p></p>
