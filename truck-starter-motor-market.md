<p><strong>The Truck Starter Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control Truck Starter Motor</li><li>Electromagnetic Control Truck Starter Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck starter motor market is divided into two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control starter motors. Direct control truck starter motors operate purely through a mechanical connection from the ignition switch, providing a straightforward and reliable option for starting engines. In contrast, electromagnetic control truck starter motors utilize an electromagnetic mechanism to engage the starter, offering enhanced efficiency and the ability to manage higher power demands. Both types cater to different vehicle applications and user preferences in commercial transport.</p></p>
