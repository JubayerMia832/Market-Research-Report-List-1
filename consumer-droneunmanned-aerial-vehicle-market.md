<p><p>The consumer drone market comprises three main types: fixed-wing drones, rotary-wing drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones utilize a traditional airplane design for extended flight duration and range, ideal for mapping and surveillance. Rotary-wing drones, commonly known as quadcopters, offer greater maneuverability and vertical takeoff, making them popular for photography and recreational use. Hybrid drones combine elements of both, providing the advantages of vertical takeoff and efficient forward flight, suitable for diverse applications and enhancing versatility in various environments.</p></p>
