<p><strong>The PVC Artificial Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calender PVC Leather</li><li>Rotary Screen Coating PVC Artificial Leather</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC artificial leather market is primarily divided into two types: calender PVC leather and rotary screen coating PVC leather. Calender PVC leather is produced by passing PVC through heated rollers, resulting in a smooth, uniform texture suitable for various applications like upholstery and fashion. Rotary screen coating PVC leather, on the other hand, involves applying PVC onto a substrate using a rotary screen process, allowing for intricate designs and patterns, making it ideal for decorative and specialized uses.</p></p>
