<p><p>The dome security camera market comprises two main types: analog cameras and IP cameras. Analog cameras transmit video signals over coaxial cables, offering a cost-effective solution with simpler installation but limited features. In contrast, IP cameras utilize network connections to deliver high-definition video and advanced capabilities such as remote access, cloud storage, and analytics. IP cameras generally provide superior image quality and flexibility, making them increasingly popular for modern security systems, while analog cameras remain a budget-friendly option for basic surveillance needs.</p></p>
