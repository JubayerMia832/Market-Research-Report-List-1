<p><strong>The Tile Levelling Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wedge System</li><li>Screw Cap Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tile leveling kits facilitate the installation of tiles by ensuring an even surface and preventing lippage. The two primary types are Wedge Systems and Screw Cap Systems. Wedge Systems use a plastic spacer and a wedge to create tension, aligning the tiles uniformly. In contrast, Screw Cap Systems employ caps that screw down to hold the tiles in place. Both systems enhance the efficiency of tile installations, allowing for a professional finish and reducing the risk of unevenness post-installation.</p></p>
