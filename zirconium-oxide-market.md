<p><ul><li>Electro Fused Zirconium Oxide</li><li>Chemical Zirconium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconium oxide market is primarily divided into two types: electro-fused zirconium oxide and chemical zirconium oxide. Electro-fused zirconium oxide is produced through high-temperature melting and is known for its high purity and durability, making it suitable for industrial applications like refractory materials and advanced ceramics. In contrast, chemical zirconium oxide is derived through chemical processes and is utilized predominantly in applications such as pigments, catalysts, and biomedical devices, due to its chemical stability and bio-compatibility.</p></p>
