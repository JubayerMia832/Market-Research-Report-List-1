<p><p>The rice milk market can be categorized into two main types: powdered rice milk and fluid rice milk. Powdered rice milk is a dehydrated form, often used for convenience, where consumers can reconstitute it by adding water, making it ideal for travel or varied applications in cooking. Fluid rice milk, on the other hand, is a ready-to-drink beverage that is typically fortified with vitamins and minerals. It caters to those seeking a lactose-free alternative to dairy milk for direct consumption or mixing in recipes.</p></p>
