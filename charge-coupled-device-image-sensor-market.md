<p><strong>The Charge-Coupled Device Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Transfer Image Sensor</li><li>Full Frame Transfer Image Sensor</li><li>Interline Transfer Image Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) image sensor market comprises several types: Frame Transfer Image Sensors, which utilize a two-part design to transfer captured images quickly and reduce readout time; Full Frame Transfer Image Sensors, known for high image quality and sensitivity, as they expose the entire sensor area at once; and Interline Transfer Image Sensors, which allow simultaneous exposure and readout by integrating vertical storage, making them ideal for video recording and fast-moving subjects. Each type serves distinct imaging applications.</p></p>
