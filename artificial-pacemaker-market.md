<p><p>The artificial pacemaker market comprises several types of pacing systems. Temporary cardiac pacing is used for short-term management of patients with transient heart issues, often in acute settings. Single chamber cardiac pacing involves a single lead positioned in either the right atrium or ventricle, suitable for simpler cases. Double-chamber cardiac pacing employs two leads, enhancing synchrony between atrial and ventricular contractions, benefiting patients with more complex arrhythmias. Other types include biventricular pacing for heart failure management and advanced devices with integrated features.</p></p>
