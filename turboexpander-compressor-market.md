<p><strong>The Turboexpander Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Turboexpander</li><li>Axial Turboexpander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboexpander compressor market is primarily categorized into two types: radial turboexpanders and axial turboexpanders. Radial turboexpanders utilize a radial flow design, optimizing efficiency in compact spaces and are often used in smaller applications like refrigeration and gas processing. Conversely, axial turboexpanders feature an axial flow design, allowing for higher throughput and efficiency in larger systems, making them suitable for power generation and industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles in energy recovery and process optimization across various industries.</p></p>
