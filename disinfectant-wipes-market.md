<p><p>The disinfectant wipes market is primarily divided into two main types: alcohol-free disinfectant wipes and alcohol disinfectant wipes. Alcohol-based wipes typically contain a high concentration of isopropyl or ethyl alcohol, making them effective at quickly killing germs and viruses. They are often used in healthcare settings and public spaces. Conversely, alcohol-free disinfectant wipes utilize alternative agents, such as quaternary ammonium compounds, appealing to users seeking less harsh options, especially in households with children or sensitive environments. Each type serves specific user preferences and needs in hygiene and cleanliness.</p></p>
