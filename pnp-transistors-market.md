<p><p>PNP transistors are a type of bipolar junction transistor (BJT) that consists of a layer of n-doped semiconductor material sandwiched between two layers of p-doped material. <p><p>The PNP transistor market primarily focuses on two main types: bipolar transistors and field-effect transistors (FETs). Bipolar transistors utilize both electron and hole charge carriers, offering high current gain, making them ideal for amplification and switching applications. Field-effect transistors, on the other hand, control current flow through an electric field, providing benefits like high input impedance and low power consumption. Both types play integral roles in electronic circuits, serving diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
