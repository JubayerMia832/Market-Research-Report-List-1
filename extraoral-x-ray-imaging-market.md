<p><p>The extraoral X-ray imaging market consists primarily of two types: panoramic X-rays and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT). Panoramic X-rays provide a broad view of the entire mouth, including teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures, aiding in dental diagnostics and treatment planning. CBCT, on the other hand, offers three-dimensional imaging, which enhances the visualization of complex dental anatomy and pathologies, proving invaluable for procedures such as implants and orthodontics. Both types are essential for comprehensive dental care.</p></p>
