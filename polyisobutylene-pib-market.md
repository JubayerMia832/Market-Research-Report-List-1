<p><p>Polyisobutylene (PIB) is a versatile synthetic polymer categorized by molecular weight into three main types: Low Molecular Weight (LMW), Medium Molecular Weight (MMW), and High Molecular Weight (HMW) PIB. LMW PIB is liquid, used in adhesives, sealants, and lubricants due to its flexibility and tackiness. MMW PIB offers a balance of viscosity and strength, ideal for coatings and fuel additives. HMW PIB is solid, providing durability and elasticity, making it suitable for automotive parts, roofing membranes, and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct industries based on its properties.</p></p>
