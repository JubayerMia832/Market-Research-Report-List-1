<p><p>The power semiconductor discrete devices market encompasses several key components: </p><p>1. **Diodes** allow current to flow in one direction, serving in rectification and voltage regulation.</p><p>2. **IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors)** combine the advantages of MOSFETs and BJTs, ideal for high-power applications.</p><p>3. **MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors)** are favored for their fast switching capabilities in low to medium power applications.</p><p>4. **BJTs (Bipolar Junction Transistors)** are used for amplifying or switching signals in various electronic devices. </p><p>5. **Thyristors** enable control of large currents and voltages, commonly applied in power electronics and motor control systems.</p></p>
