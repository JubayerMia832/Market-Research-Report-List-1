<p><ul><li>Electrode Type Blood Glucose Meter</li><li>Photoelectric Blood Glucose Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood glucose meter market comprises various types, primarily including electrode type and photoelectric blood glucose meters. Electrode type meters utilize electrochemical sensing to measure glucose levels through blood samples, providing quick and precise readings. In contrast, photoelectric meters rely on light absorption or reflection to analyze glucose concentration, offering a non-invasive or less invasive approach. Both technologies serve the critical function of helping individuals with diabetes monitor their blood sugar levels effectively, enhancing their ability to manage their condition.</p></p>
