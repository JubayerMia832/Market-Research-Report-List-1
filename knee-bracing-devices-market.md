<p><strong>The Knee Bracing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prophylactic Braces</li><li>Functional Braces</li><li>Rehabilitative Braces</li><li>Unloader Braces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knee bracing devices are categorized into four main types: Prophylactic braces, designed to prevent injuries during sports; Functional braces, which support knees that have already been injured, enhancing stability and mobility; Rehabilitative braces, used during recovery to limit harmful movements while promoting healing; and Unloader braces, specifically engineered to relieve pain by redistributing weight across the knee joint, often used for arthritis patients. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying needs for protection, support, healing, and pain management.</p></p>
