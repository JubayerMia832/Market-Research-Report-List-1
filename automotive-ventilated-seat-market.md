<p><p>The automotive ventilated seat market encompasses two primary types: suction type and air supply type. Suction type systems use fans to draw air away from the seat's surface, cooling the occupant by creating airflow. In contrast, air supply type systems push conditioned air into the seat, enhancing comfort by promoting circulation. Both types aim to improve passenger experience, particularly in hot weather, while providing adjustable settings to suit individual preferences, contributing to heightened safety and comfort in vehicles.</p></p>
