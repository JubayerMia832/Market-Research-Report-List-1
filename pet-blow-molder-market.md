<p><p>The PET blow molder market comprises three main types: Extrusion Blow Molding Machines, which create hollow plastic parts through continuous extrusion; Injection Blow Molding Machines, where pre-formed parts are injected and then blown into shape; and Stretch Blow Molding Machines, which stretch the preform during blowing for enhanced clarity and strength. Each type serves different applications in producing PET containers, catering to diverse industrial needs in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
