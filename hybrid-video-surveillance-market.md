<p><p>The hybrid video surveillance market encompasses two primary types: Analog Video Surveillance Systems and IP Video Surveillance Systems. Analog systems utilize traditional coaxial cables and CCTV cameras to transmit video signals, offering a cost-effective solution for basic monitoring needs. In contrast, IP Video Surveillance Systems use internet protocol technology, enabling higher resolution imaging, remote access, and advanced features such as analytics and integration with other security systems. Together, these types address varying security requirements and technology advancements in video surveillance.</p></p>
