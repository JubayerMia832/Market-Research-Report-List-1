<p><p>The Diesel Vehicle Exhaust System market includes two primary types: Single Exhaust System and Dual Exhaust System. A Single Exhaust System uses one pipe to expel gases, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, ideal for smaller or less powerful vehicles. Dual Exhaust Systems feature two separate pipes, enhancing performance, fuel efficiency, and sound quality, commonly used in high-performance or larger diesel vehicles. Both systems aim to reduce emissions, improve engine efficiency, and comply with environmental regulations, catering to different vehicle needs and preferences.</p></p>
