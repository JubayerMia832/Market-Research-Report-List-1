<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma Spectrometer (ICP) is an analytical technique used for the detection and quantification of trace elements in various samples. It utilizes high-temperature plasma to ionize the sample, allowing for the measurement of elemental concentrations through optical emission or mass spectrometry. This method is highly regarded for its sensitivity, precision, and ability to analyze multiple elements simultaneously, making it essential in sectors such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and metallurgy.</p><p>The Inductively Coupled Plasma Spectrometer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period.
