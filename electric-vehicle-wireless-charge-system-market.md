Magnetic Resonance Technology enables charging over longer distances with higher efficiency, using resonant coils to transfer energy. Inductive Charging Technology relies on electromagnetic fields for close-range energy transfer, requiring precise alignment between the vehicle and charging pad. Both systems eliminate the need for physical connectors, offering convenience and reducing wear.
