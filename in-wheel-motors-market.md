<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Type</li><li>Outer Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-wheel motors market is primarily categorized into two types: inner rotor type and outer rotor type. </p><p>Inner rotor type motors feature the rotor located inside the stator, offering a compact design and efficiency. They excel in torque generation and are often favored in applications requiring high performance. </p><p>In contrast, outer rotor type motors place the rotor on the outside, providing a larger surface area for heat dissipation. This design enhances cooling and is beneficial for larger vehicles, allowing for improved stability and greater power output.</p></p>
