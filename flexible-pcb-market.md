<p><strong>The Flexible PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li><li>Multi-Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible PCB market encompasses three main types: single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single-layer PCBs have one conductive layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for less complex applications. Double-layer PCBs feature two conductive layers, allowing for more intricate designs and enhanced functionality. Multi-layer PCBs consist of three or more layers, providing superior routing options and higher density, making them ideal for advanced electronics in industries like telecommunications and consumer electronics. Each type serves specific needs based on complexity and application.</p></p>
