<p><p>The Non-network Connected Non-IC Card Power Smart Meter market features two primary types: Simplex and Three Phase smart meters. Simplex meters are designed for single-phase applications, typically used in residential settings, enabling straightforward energy monitoring and billing. In contrast, Three Phase meters cater to industrial and commercial environments, measuring power consumption across three phases for enhanced accuracy and efficiency. Both types facilitate energy management without relying on network connections or integrated circuit cards, offering a cost-effective solution for diverse electricity consumption needs.</p></p>
