<p><p>The bariatric surgical devices market comprises various procedures aimed at treating obesity. Gastric banding involves placing an adjustable band around the stomach to restrict food intake. Other surgeries include procedures like biliopancreatic diversion and intragastric balloons, offering diverse options for patients with varying needs and preferences in their weight loss journeys.</p></p>
