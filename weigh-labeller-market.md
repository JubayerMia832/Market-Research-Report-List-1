<p><strong>The Weigh Labeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Fully automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weigh labeller market is segmented into semi-automatic and fully automatic systems. Semi-automatic weigh labellers require manual intervention for product placement and labeling, making them suitable for low to medium production volumes. They offer flexibility and are cost-effective for smaller businesses. In contrast, fully automatic weigh labellers operate independently, handling product counting, weighing, and labeling at high speeds, which enhances efficiency and minimizes labor costs. These systems cater to high-volume industries, ensuring accuracy and throughput in packaging lines.</p></p>
