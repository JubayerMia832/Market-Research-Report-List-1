<p><ul><li>Spunmelt Nonwovens</li><li>Spunbonded Nonwovens</li><li>Spunlace Nonwovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nonwovens for Hygiene market includes various types like Spunmelt, Spunbonded, and Spunlace nonwovens. Spunmelt nonwovens are created by fusing layers of meltblown and spunbond fibers, offering softness and liquid barrier properties, ideal for diapers and feminine hygiene products. Spunbonded nonwovens are made from continuous filaments, providing strength and durability, commonly used in various hygiene applications. Spunlace nonwoven fabrics are produced through a hydroentangling process, giving a soft texture suitable for wipes and personal care items.</p></p>
