<p><p>The dirt bike tire market includes three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering superior shock absorption and traction, ideal for off-road terrain. Solid tires, made of rubber or foam, are puncture-proof and durable, suited for heavy-duty or industrial use. Polyurethane tires are lightweight, resistant to wear, and often used in specialized applications where low rolling resistance is key. Each type caters to specific riding conditions, balancing performance, durability, and maintenance needs.</p></p>
