<p><p>The probe card market is segmented into three main types: Cantilever Probe Cards, Vertical Probe Cards, and MEMS Probe Cards. Cantilever Probe Cards utilize a cantilever design for efficient contact with wafer pads, ideal for high-volume production. Vertical Probe Cards feature an upright configuration, providing precise alignment for dense packaging applications. MEMS Probe Cards leverage micro-electromechanical systems technology for enhanced performance and scalability, offering customizable solutions for advanced semiconductor testing, including finer pitch requirements. Each type serves distinct market needs and applications.</p></p>
