<p><p>The bio-herbicides market includes three main types: microbials, biochemicals, and others. Microbials consist of living organisms, such as bacteria and fungi, that target and suppress weed growth naturally. Biochemicals are derived from natural plant extracts and other organic materials, functioning through biochemical mechanisms to inhibit weed germination and growth. The "others" category encompasses innovative approaches and products not classified as microbials or biochemicals, including plant-based compounds and synergistic formulations, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
