<p><ul><li>Single Factor Certification</li><li>Multifactor Certification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phone-based authentication encompasses two primary market types: Single Factor Certification and Multifactor Certification. Single Factor Certification relies on a single form of verification, typically a one-time password (OTP) sent to a user's phone. In contrast, Multifactor Certification enhances security by requiring multiple verification methods, such as combining an OTP with biometric data or security questions. As cyber threats increase, the demand for these authentication types grows, with multifactor solutions offering greater protection for sensitive information and transactions.</p></p>
