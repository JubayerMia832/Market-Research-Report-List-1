<p><p>Thyrectors are semiconductor devices used for controlling power in electrical circuits. The market for thyrectors can be categorized into two main types: uni-polar thyrectors and bi-polar thyrectors. Uni-polar thyrectors utilize a single polarity, typically involving a gate and anode, making them simpler and often more efficient for specific applications like dimmers and motor controls. In contrast, bi-polar thyrectors can handle both positive and negative voltages, offering greater versatility for a range of industrial and commercial applications in power management.</p></p>
