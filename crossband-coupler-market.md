<p><p>The crossband coupler market is segmented into three main types: tower-mounted, rack-mounted, and stand-alone. Tower-mounted couplers are integrated into transmission towers, enhancing signal amplification and distribution directly at the site. Rack-mounted couplers are designed for installation in server rooms or communication racks, offering organized connectivity for various equipment. Stand-alone couplers are independent units that can be placed anywhere, providing flexibility and ease of installation for various applications, making them versatile solutions for communication systems.</p></p>
