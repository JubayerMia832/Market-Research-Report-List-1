<p><p>Factory automation and industrial controls encompass technologies that streamline manufacturing processes. The market is divided into two main types: field devices and industrial control systems. Field devices include sensors, actuators, and transmitters that collect data and execute commands on the factory floor. Industrial control systems, such as SCADA and PLCs, manage and coordinate operations, ensuring efficiency and safety. Together, these components enhance productivity, reduce operational costs, and enable real-time monitoring and control of industrial processes.</p></p>
