<p><p>The Radiology Information System (RIS) market is categorized into three main types: cloud-based, install-based, and combined systems. Cloud-based RIS offers remote access and scalability, allowing facilities to manage data efficiently without extensive on-site infrastructure. Install-based RIS requires on-premises installation, providing greater control and customization, ideal for larger institutions with specific needs. Combined systems integrate both cloud and on-premises solutions, offering flexibility and maximizing benefits from both approaches, catering to diverse operational requirements in radiology departments.</p></p>
