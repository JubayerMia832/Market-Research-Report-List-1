<p><ul><li>Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process Product</li><li>Acid Washing Process Product</li><li>Heat Denaturation Process Product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy protein concentrates market includes three primary types based on their production processes. The Aqueous Alcohol Washing Process product involves using alcohol to extract proteins, resulting in a high solubility concentrate. The Acid Washing Process product utilizes acid to remove non-protein components, enhancing protein purity. Lastly, the Heat Denaturation Process product employs heat to alter protein structure, improving functionality and digestibility. Each type offers distinct benefits for food manufacturers, catering to various nutrition and formulation needs.</p></p>
