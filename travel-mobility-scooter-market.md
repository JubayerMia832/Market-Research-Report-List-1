<p><p>The Travel Mobility Scooter market is divided into two main categories: Class 2 and Class 3 scooters. Class 2 scooters are designed for pavement use and are typically limited to a maximum speed of 4 mph, making them suitable for short trips and indoor use. In contrast, Class 3 scooters can travel on roads, with speeds up to 8 mph, and often feature enhanced safety features for outdoor conditions. Both types serve to enhance mobility and independence for users with limited mobility.</p></p>
