<p><ul><li>Fluorocarbon Elastomers</li><li>Fluorosilicone Elastomers</li><li>Perfluoroelastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluoride rubber market is segmented into three main types: Fluorocarbon elastomers, which offer excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. Fluorosilicone elastomers combine the properties of silicone with fluorocarbon, providing enhanced fuel and oil resistance, suitable for the aerospace and automotive industries. Perfluoroelastomers feature superior chemical and thermal durability, often used in demanding environments like oil and gas, pharmaceuticals, and semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring reliability and longevity in performance.</p></p>
