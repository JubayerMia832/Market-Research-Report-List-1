<p><p>The C4 LLDPE film market encompasses various manufacturing methods, notably blown film extrusion and cast film extrusion. Blown film extrusion involves inflating molten polymer into a tube, creating strong, flexible films ideal for packaging. In contrast, cast film extrusion involves spreading melted polymer onto a flat surface, resulting in films with excellent clarity and uniform thickness, suitable for applications like labels and food packaging.
