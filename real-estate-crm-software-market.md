<p><p>Real Estate CRM software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering complete control and customization but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CRM systems are hosted on remote servers, providing accessibility from anywhere with internet connectivity, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. The choice between these types depends on the organization’s needs for flexibility, control, and resource availability.</p></p>
