<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Type</li><li>Thermoplastics Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engineering plastics market comprises two primary types: thermosetting and thermoplastics. Thermosetting plastics are polymers that, once cured, retain their shape and are heat-resistant, making them ideal for high-temperature applications in vehicle components. Thermoplastics, on the other hand, can be melted and reshaped multiple times, offering versatility and ease of processing for parts like dashboards and panels. Both types contribute significantly to lightweighting, durability, and design flexibility in modern automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
