<p><p>The Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly Service (OSAT) market can be categorized into two main types: Advanced Packaging and Traditional Packaging. Advanced Packaging involves sophisticated techniques, such as 3D stacking and system-in-package (SiP) technologies, which enhance performance and integration. This type caters to high-performance applications. In contrast, Traditional Packaging focuses on standard packaging methods like wire bonding and surface mount technology, primarily used for less complex semiconductor devices. Both types are essential for meeting varying technological demands and market requirements.</p></p>
