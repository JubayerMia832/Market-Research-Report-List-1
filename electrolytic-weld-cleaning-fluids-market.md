<p><p>The electrolytic weld cleaning fluids market features two primary types: water-based and solvent-based cleaning fluids. Water-based cleaning fluids utilize aqueous formulas, offering effective removal of welding contamination while being environmentally friendly and lower in toxicity. In contrast, solvent-based cleaning fluids leverage organic solvents to dissolve grime and oxidation, providing rapid cleaning capabilities but often posing greater health and environmental concerns. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, ensuring optimal weld quality and surface preparation.</p></p>
