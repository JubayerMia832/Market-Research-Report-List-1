<p><p>The door access control readers market comprises various types, including card-based readers, biometric readers, and multi-technology readers. Card-based readers utilize swipe, proximity, or smart cards for identification, offering a cost-effective solution for access management. Biometric readers enhance security by using unique physical traits, like fingerprints or facial recognition, ensuring only authorized users gain entry. Multi-technology readers combine both card and biometric systems, providing flexibility and improved security. This diverse range addresses varying needs in security, convenience, and user identification.</p></p>
