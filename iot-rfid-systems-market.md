<p><ul><li>Passive RFID System</li><li>Active RFID System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID systems market comprises two main types: passive and active RFID systems. Passive RFID systems utilize tags that do not have their own power source; they rely on the reader's electromagnetic field to activate. These are cost-effective and ideal for tracking items like inventory. Active RFID systems, however, contain their own battery, enabling them to transmit signals independently and over longer ranges. They are suitable for real-time tracking in applications such as asset management and personnel monitoring.</p></p>
