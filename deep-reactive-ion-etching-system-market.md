<p><p>The Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) system market includes two primary processes: the Bosch process and the Cryo process. The Bosch process involves alternating between etching and passivation steps, enabling high aspect ratio features with smooth sidewalls, ideal for microfabrication applications. In contrast, the Cryo process utilizes extremely low temperatures to achieve better depth control and minimize redeposition, resulting in improved feature quality. Both processes cater to industries like semiconductor manufacturing, MEMS, and advanced packaging, driving demand for innovative etching solutions.</p></p>
