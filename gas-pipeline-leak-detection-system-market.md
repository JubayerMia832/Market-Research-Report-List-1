<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensors</li><li>Flow Meters</li><li>Fibre Optic Sensors</li><li>Acoustic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas pipeline leak detection system market encompasses various technologies, including pressure sensors, flow meters, fibre optic sensors, and acoustic sensors. Pressure sensors monitor variations in pipeline pressure to identify potential leaks. Flow meters assess discrepancies between expected and actual gas flow rates. Fibre optic sensors use light transmission to detect changes in temperature or strain, indicating leaks. Acoustic sensors capture sound waves produced by gas escaping from a pipeline, allowing for precise leak localization. Each technology plays a critical role in ensuring pipeline safety and integrity.</p></p>
