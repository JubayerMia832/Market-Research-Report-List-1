<p><p>The obstetric delivery tables market comprises two main types: electric and manual tables. Electric obstetric delivery tables offer automated adjustments for height, backrest, and lithotomy position, enhancing precision and ease of use during delivery. They typically include features like integrated lighting and electronic controls, improving efficiency and comfort. Manual obstetric delivery tables, on the other hand, rely on manual adjustments for positioning and height, providing a cost-effective alternative but requiring more physical effort from healthcare providers during procedures.</p></p>
