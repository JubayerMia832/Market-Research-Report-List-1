<p><ul><li>Linear Robot Actuators</li><li>Rotary Robot Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robot actuators market is primarily divided into linear and rotary types. Linear robot actuators facilitate straight-line motion, often used in applications like pick and place, assembly, and packaging. They typically employ mechanisms such as screws, belts, or pneumatic systems. In contrast, rotary robot actuators provide rotational motion, essential for tasks such as welding, painting, and precision manipulation. Both types are crucial in enhancing efficiency and precision in automation across various industries, including manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
