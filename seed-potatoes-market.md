<p><ul><li>Conventional Seed Potatoes</li><li>Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seed potatoes market consists primarily of two main types: conventional seed potatoes and micro propagation seed potatoes. Conventional seed potatoes are produced through traditional farming methods, utilizing seeds grown in the field, which can be more susceptible to diseases. In contrast, micro propagation seed potatoes are cultivated in a controlled laboratory environment using tissue culture techniques, ensuring higher quality, disease-free plants and faster multiplication rates. Both types serve different agricultural needs and contribute to overall potato production efficiency.</p></p>
