<p><p>The Industrial I/O Modules market comprises three main types: PLC I/O Modules, DCS I/O Modules, and PC-based I/O Modules. PLC I/O Modules facilitate communication between a programmable logic controller and field devices for automation tasks. DCS I/O Modules are designed for distributed control systems, enabling real-time monitoring and control of processes across multiple locations. PC-based I/O Modules offer flexibility and high processing capabilities, connecting sensors and actuators directly to PCs for advanced data analysis and control in industrial environments.</p></p>
