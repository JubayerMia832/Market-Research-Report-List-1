<p><p>The spreadable industrial margarine market is categorized into hard and soft types based on texture and application. Hard margarine is firmer, suitable for baking and cooking, providing stability in high-temperature processes. It typically has a lower water content, enhancing its shelf life. In contrast, soft margarine is designed for spreading, with a creamier texture ideal for table use. It contains higher moisture content, making it easy to spread on bread and pastries, catering to consumer preferences in various culinary applications.</p></p>
