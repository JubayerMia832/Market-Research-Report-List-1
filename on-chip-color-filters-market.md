<p><p>The On-Chip Color Filters market consists of several types, including the Bayer Filter, RGBE Filter, RYYB Filter, and others. The Bayer Filter, with its red, green, and blue pixel arrangement, is widely used in image sensors for capturing color images. The RGBE Filter incorporates an additional emerald pixel for enhanced green sensitivity. The RYYB Filter replaces one of the green pixels with yellow, improving low-light performance. Other filters encompass various innovative designs aimed at optimizing image quality and sensor efficiency.</p></p>
