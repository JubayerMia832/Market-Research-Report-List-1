<p><p>The passenger vehicle turbocharger market includes two main types: Diesel Engine Turbochargers and Gasoline Engine Turbochargers. Diesel turbochargers enhance engine efficiency by compressing air, improving combustion in diesel engines, which are known for high torque and fuel efficiency. Gasoline turbochargers, on the other hand, boost power output in gasoline engines by increasing air intake, enabling smaller engines to deliver higher performance while maintaining fuel economy. Both types aim to optimize engine performance, reduce emissions, and meet stringent environmental regulations.</p></p>
