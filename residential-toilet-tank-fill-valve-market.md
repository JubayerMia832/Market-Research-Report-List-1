<p><strong>The Residential Toilet Tank Fill Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Ball</li><li>Float Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential toilet tank fill valve market consists primarily of two types: float ball and float cup mechanisms. Float ball valves utilize a buoyant ball attached to a lever, which rises and falls with the water level, controlling the flow of water into the tank. In contrast, float cup valves feature a cylindrical float that moves vertically along a rod, providing precise water level regulation. Both designs aim to refill the toilet tank efficiently while preventing overflow, catering to consumer preferences for reliability and functionality.</p></p>
