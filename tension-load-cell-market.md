<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Load Cell</li><li>Hydraulic Load Cell</li><li>Pneumatic Load Cell</li><li>Other Types</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tension load cell market encompasses several types, each utilizing different principles for measuring force. Piezoelectric load cells generate an electrical charge in response to applied tension, offering high accuracy and fast response times. Hydraulic load cells use fluid pressure to determine force, providing robustness in harsh environments. Pneumatic load cells operate on air pressure changes, suitable for lightweight applications. Additionally, other types may include strain gauge and capacitive load cells, each tailored for specific industrial requirements and applications.</p></p>
