<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rubber Grade Petroleum Resin market consists of three primary types: C5, C9, and C5/C9 petroleum resins. C5 petroleum resin, derived from pentene fractions, offers excellent adhesion and flexibility, making it ideal for adhesives and sealants. C9 petroleum resin, sourced from aromatic fractions, provides higher softening points and thermal stability, suited for various industrial applications. C5/C9 petroleum resin combines features of both types, delivering a balanced performance in adhesives, coatings, and rubber applications, appealing to diverse manufacturing needs.</p></p>
