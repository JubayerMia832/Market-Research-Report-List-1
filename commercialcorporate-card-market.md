<p><ul><li>Open-Loop</li><li>Closed Loop Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial/Corporate Card market comprises two main types: Open-Loop and Closed-Loop cards. Open-Loop cards can be used at multiple merchants, giving businesses flexibility and greater acceptance. They typically operate on major card networks, allowing for extensive usage across various sectors. Closed-Loop cards, on the other hand, are restricted to specific retailers or service providers, offering deeper integration and tailored solutions within a closed ecosystem. This can enhance tracking and control over expenses for corporations, catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
