<p><strong>The Revision Knee Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cemented Revision Knee Replacement</li><li>Cementless Revision Knee Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The revision knee replacement market is primarily categorized into two types: cemented and cementless revision procedures. Cemented revision knee replacements involve the use of bone cement to secure the implant to the bone, providing immediate stability and support. This method is commonly used for patients with sufficient bone quality. In contrast, cementless revision knee replacements allow for bone integration with the implant, relying on the porous surface of the implant to promote natural bone growth, making it suitable for patients with compromised bone integrity.</p></p>
