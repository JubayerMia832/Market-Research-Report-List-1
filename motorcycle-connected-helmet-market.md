<p><p>The motorcycle connected helmet market is divided into two main types: freestanding and embedded helmets. Freestanding helmets incorporate external devices, such as Bluetooth units, allowing riders to connect to smartphones and GPS systems without altering the helmet's structure. In contrast, embedded helmets feature built-in technologies, such as speakers, microphones, and display screens, offering a seamless integration of communication and navigation functions. Both types enhance rider safety and experience but differ in their design and technological integration methods.</p></p>
