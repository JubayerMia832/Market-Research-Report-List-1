<p><p>The cationic aqueous polyurethane market is primarily categorized into two types based on their functional groups: carboxylic acid type polyurethane and sulfonic acid type polyurethane. Carboxylic acid type polyurethanes incorporate carboxyl groups, enhancing adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Sulfonic acid type polyurethanes contain sulfonic acid groups, which improve solubility and stability in aqueous environments, making them ideal for applications in textiles and paper coatings. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
