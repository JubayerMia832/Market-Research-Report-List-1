<p><p>The extensional rheometer system market is primarily categorized into dynamic and capillary types. Dynamic extensional rheometers measure the material's response to time-dependent strain rates, evaluating properties under various conditions. Capillary extensional rheometers assess fluid behavior through a narrow channel, focusing on steady-state flow characteristics. Other types may include novel designs or applications tailored to specific materials or industries, enhancing the understanding of fluid behavior in various processing conditions and aiding in material development and quality control.</p></p>
