<p><p>Hospital linen supply and management services encompass two primary market types: the rental system and the customer-owned goods market. The rental system involves hospitals outsourcing their linen needs to specialized vendors who provide, clean, and manage linens on a lease basis. This allows for cost efficiency and reduced management burdens. In contrast, the customer-owned goods market involves hospitals purchasing their linens outright and managing laundry services in-house, requiring more resources but allowing for greater control over inventory and quality.</p></p>
