<p><p>The Cloud Platform as a Service (PaaS) software market is divided into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based. In contrast, cloud-based PaaS leverages remote servers and infrastructure provided by a third-party vendor, ensuring scalability, accessibility, and reduced overhead costs.
