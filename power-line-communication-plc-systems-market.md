<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) systems utilize existing electrical wiring to transmit data alongside electrical power. The market is divided into narrowband and broadband PLC types. Narrowband PLC focuses on low data rates for applications like smart metering and remote control, making it ideal for long-distance communication. In contrast, broadband PLC offers higher data rates suitable for internet access and multimedia applications, catering to urban environments where demand for high-speed connectivity is growing. Both markets leverage the infrastructure of power lines for enhanced communication solutions.</p></p>
