<p><strong>The IoT RFID Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Labels</li><li>Active Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID labels market is primarily categorized into passive and active labels. Passive labels do not have their own power source and rely on electromagnetic energy from RFID readers to operate, making them cost-effective and suitable for applications like inventory management. In contrast, active labels are equipped with their own power source, enabling them to transmit signals over longer distances and store more data. These are ideal for real-time tracking and monitoring in logistics and supply chain management.</p></p>
