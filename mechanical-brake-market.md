Electromagnetic brakes utilize electromagnetic forces to engage and disengage braking, offering quick response and reliability, often found in electric vehicles and industrial machinery. Conversely, mechanical hydraulic brakes use hydraulic fluid to amplify force and provide effective stopping power, commonly used in automotive and heavy machinery applications. Both systems are essential for ensuring safety and control in various transportation and industrial environments, each with unique operational advantages.</p></p>
