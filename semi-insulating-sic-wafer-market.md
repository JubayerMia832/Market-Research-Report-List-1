<p><ul><li>4H</li><li>6H</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-insulating SiC wafer market is primarily categorized into two types: 4H-SiC and 6H-SiC. 4H-SiC wafers feature a hexagonal crystal structure that enhances electron mobility, making them suitable for high-power and high-frequency applications. In contrast, 6H-SiC wafers exhibit a different crystalline arrangement, which generally provides greater thermal conductivity but lower electron mobility. Both types serve crucial roles in power electronics, RF devices, and optoelectronic applications, driving advancements in energy efficiency and device performance.</p></p>
