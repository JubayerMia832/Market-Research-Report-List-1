<p><ul><li>Hydraulic-Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Power Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-propelled belt loader market features three primary types based on their transmission systems. Hydraulic-mechanical transmission combines hydraulic power and mechanical components for efficient loading operations. Hydraulic transmission relies solely on hydraulic systems, providing smooth and adjustable power delivery, ideal for various terrains. Power transmission focuses on using mechanical methods, often with gears and motors, to drive the belt loader. Each type caters to specific operational needs in logistics and cargo handling, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
