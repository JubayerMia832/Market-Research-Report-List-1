<p><p>The Miniature Current and Voltage Transformers market encompasses two primary types: Miniature Current Transformers (MCTs) and Miniature Voltage Transformers (MVTs). MCTs are designed to accurately measure and convert primary current levels into manageable lower levels for instrumentation or protection devices, ensuring safety and efficiency in electrical systems. MVTs serve a similar function for voltage, enabling precise voltage measurement and monitoring in compact applications. Both types are essential for optimizing power management, enhancing system reliability, and facilitating maintenance in various industries.</p></p>
