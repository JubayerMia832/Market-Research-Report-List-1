<p><p>The protective surgical mask market can be categorized into two main types: flat-fold and cup type. Flat-fold masks are designed for easy storage and transport, featuring a flexible design that allows them to be folded flat when not in use. In contrast, cup-type masks have a rigid, three-dimensional shape that provides a better fit and enhanced facial coverage, offering improved protection against airborne particles. Both types serve essential roles in healthcare and various industries, ensuring safety for users.</p></p>
