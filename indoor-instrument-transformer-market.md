<p><ul><li>Current Transformer</li><li>Potential Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor instrument transformer market primarily includes current transformers (CTs) and potential transformers (PTs). Current transformers are used to measure and monitor electrical currents in high-voltage systems, providing accurate data for protection and control. Potential transformers, on the other hand, step down voltage levels to safer values for measurement and monitoring in power systems. Both types are essential for ensuring the reliable operation of electrical equipment, enhancing safety, and improving efficiency in electrical installations and substations.</p></p>
