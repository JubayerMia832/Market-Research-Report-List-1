<p><p>The Multi Pixel Photon Counter (MPPC) market is divided into two primary types based on detection capabilities: Single Photon Detection and Multiphoton Detection. Single Photon Detection focuses on capturing individual photons, making it ideal for applications in low-light environments and quantum optics. In contrast, Multiphoton Detection is designed to detect multiple photons simultaneously, enhancing sensitivity and speed, which is crucial for advanced imaging techniques and high-energy physics experiments. Both types play vital roles in fields like medical diagnostics, telecommunications, and research.</p></p>
