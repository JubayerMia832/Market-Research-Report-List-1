<p><p>The conductive FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container) market is divided into two main types: woven and non-woven. Woven conductive FIBCs are made from interlaced fibers, offering strength and durability, making them suitable for handling hazardous materials like electronics. Non-woven conductive FIBCs, on the other hand, are crafted from bonded fibers, providing lighter weight and better flexibility, ideal for applications where intricate shapes or packaging is necessary. Both types effectively mitigate static electricity, enhancing safety in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
