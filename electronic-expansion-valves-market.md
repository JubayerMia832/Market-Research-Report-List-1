<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Expansion Valves</li><li>Electric Expansion Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic expansion valves (EEVs) market comprises primarily two types: electromagnetic expansion valves and electric expansion valves. Electromagnetic expansion valves utilize a solenoid to control refrigerant flow, reacting to various system pressures, while electric expansion valves employ a stepper motor or servo mechanism for precise control. Both types enhance energy efficiency and optimize refrigeration systems by providing accurate flow regulation. Their adoption is growing across HVAC and refrigeration applications, driven by increasing demands for energy conservation and performance improvement.</p></p>
