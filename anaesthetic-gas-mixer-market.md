<p><p>The anaesthetic gas mixer market comprises two main types: high-pressure blenders and filling blenders. High-pressure blenders are designed to accurately mix gases at high pressures, ensuring the precise delivery of anaesthetic agents during surgeries. Filling blenders, on the other hand, focus on the efficient and safe filling of anaesthetic gas cylinders, maintaining the correct ratios and minimizing wastage. Both types are essential for effective anaesthesia delivery, enhancing patient safety and operational efficiency in medical settings.</p></p>
