<p><p>The Remote Radio Unit (RRU) market is essential for integrated and distributed base station applications. Conversely, distributed base stations utilize RRUs positioned remotely from the baseband unit, enabling improved coverage and capacity in challenging environments. Both configurations facilitate enhanced signal quality, lower latency, and support for advanced technologies like 5G, driving demand for RRUs in modern telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
