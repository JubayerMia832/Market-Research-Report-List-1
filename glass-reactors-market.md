<p><ul><li>Thin Film Evaporators</li><li>Short Path Evaporators</li><li>Filter Reactors</li><li>Pressure Reactors & Rotary Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass reactors market encompasses various specialized types designed for chemical processes. Thin film evaporators facilitate efficient solvent separation through a thin liquid film, while short path evaporators utilize minimal distance for vapor transport, ensuring gentle handling of sensitive compounds. Filter reactors combine reaction and filtration in one unit for streamlined processes, and pressure reactors operate under elevated pressures for enhanced reactions. Rotary evaporators enable solvent removal through continuous rotation, optimizing evaporation rates. These technologies cater to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and research industries.</p></p>
