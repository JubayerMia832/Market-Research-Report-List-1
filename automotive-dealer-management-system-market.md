<p><p>The Automotive Dealer Management System (DMS) market is primarily divided into two types: on-site and cloud-based solutions. On-site DMS involves installing software directly on dealership servers, offering control and customization while requiring significant IT maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based DMS is hosted online, allowing dealerships to access data anytime, anywhere, with automatic updates and reduced IT costs. Both types cater to the unique operational needs of dealerships, enhancing efficiency and streamlining processes for vehicle sales, service, and inventory management.</p></p>
