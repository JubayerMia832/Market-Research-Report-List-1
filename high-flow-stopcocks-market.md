<p><p>The high flow stopcocks market consists of various types designed for different medical applications. The 2-way stopcock is simpler, allowing control between two lines, while the 1-way stopcock offers a single flow route, primarily for unidirectional control. Each type serves specific needs in healthcare settings, optimizing fluid delivery and management.</p></p>
