<p><strong>The DUV Lithography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Submerged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DUV lithography machines are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, categorized into dry and submerged types. Dry DUV lithography utilizes a dry lens system for exposure, suitable for less complex patterns but facing limitations in resolution. Submerged DUV lithography, on the other hand, employs immersion fluids to enhance resolution and enable smaller feature sizes, making it ideal for advanced nodes. The choice between these technologies depends on fabrication requirements, with submerged lithography favored for cutting-edge chip designs.</p></p>
