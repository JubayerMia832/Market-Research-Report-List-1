<p><strong>The Smart Sprinkler Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weather-Based Controllers</li><li>Sensor-Based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart sprinkler controller market is primarily divided into two types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust irrigation schedules, optimizing water usage based on precipitation and temperature forecasts. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors to gauge the actual moisture content in the ground, activating irrigation only when necessary. Together, these technologies promote efficient water management, conserve resources, and enhance the health of landscapes while minimizing water waste.</p></p>
