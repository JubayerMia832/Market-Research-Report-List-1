<p><p>The Automotive Active Seat Belt market includes various types designed for enhanced safety and comfort. **Two-point belts** secure the lap, common in older vehicles. **Three-point belts** combine lap and shoulder straps, widely used in modern cars. **Four-point belts** add an extra shoulder strap, offering better restraint. **Five-point belts** include crotch straps, ideal for racing and child seats. **Six-point belts** provide additional leg straps for extreme safety. **Seven-point belts** offer maximum restraint with extra anchoring points, used in specialized applications. Each type caters to specific safety needs and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
