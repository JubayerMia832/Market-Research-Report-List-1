<p><p>The medical protective masks market consists of two primary types: disposable masks and reusable masks. Disposable masks are designed for single-use, providing a convenient option for healthcare settings where hygiene is critical. They are typically made from non-woven materials and offer effective filtration against pathogens. Reusable masks, on the other hand, are crafted from durable fabrics and can be washed and sterilized for repeated use. These masks tend to be more sustainable and cost-effective over time but require proper maintenance to ensure ongoing efficacy.</p></p>
