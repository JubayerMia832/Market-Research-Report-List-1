<p><strong>The MEMS Inkjet Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet</li><li>Random Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS inkjet heads encompass two primary market types: continuous inkjet and random inkjet. Continuous inkjet technology delivers a constant stream of ink droplets, allowing for high-speed printing and the ability to print on various surfaces. In contrast, random inkjet, or drop-on-demand technology, releases droplets only when needed, offering precise control and reducing ink wastage. Both types serve diverse applications, from industrial printing to consumer products, with advancements in MEMS technology enhancing their effectiveness and efficiency.</p></p>
