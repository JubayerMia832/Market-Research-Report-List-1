<p><strong>The Low-E (emissivity) Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Low-E Glass</li><li>Double Low-E Glass</li><li>Triple Low-E Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low-E glass market consists of three primary types based on the number of glass panes and their insulating properties. Single Low-E glass features a single pane coated with a low-emissivity layer, enhancing thermal performance but offering limited insulation. Double Low-E glass incorporates two panes with low-E coatings on one or both surfaces, providing improved insulation and energy efficiency. Triple Low-E glass adds another pane, maximizing thermal resistance and efficiency, making it ideal for energy-conscious buildings. Each type caters to varying energy savings and insulation needs.</p></p>
