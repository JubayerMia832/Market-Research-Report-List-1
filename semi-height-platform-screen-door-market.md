<p><p>The semi-height platform screen door market is categorized into two main types: electric control and pneumatic control. Electric control systems utilize electric motors to operate the doors, offering precise and quick responses. In contrast, pneumatic control systems employ compressed air to open and close the doors, providing a reliable and efficient option, often preferred in environments where electricity supply may be inconsistent. Both systems enhance passenger safety and comfort while streamlining train operations and improving platform management.</p></p>
