<p><p>The Vehicle Keyless Go market includes Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicle without physically using a key, relying on proximity sensors and automatic authentication. RKES, on the other hand, requires users to press a button on a remote fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more seamless, hands-free experience compared to RKES.</p></p>
