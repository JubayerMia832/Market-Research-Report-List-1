<p><p>Cleanroom tacky mats are essential for minimizing contamination in controlled environments. The market is divided into two main types: temporary tacky mats and permanent tacky mats. Temporary tacky mats are designed for short-term use, often featuring peel-off layers that reveal a fresh adhesive surface, making them ideal for events or construction sites. In contrast, permanent tacky mats are durable, designed for long-term use in cleanrooms or laboratories, and can withstand repeated cleaning and maintenance while consistently reducing particulate transfer.</p></p>
