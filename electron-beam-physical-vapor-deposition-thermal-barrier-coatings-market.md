<p><p>Electron Beam Physical Vapor Deposition (EB-PVD) is a sophisticated coating process widely utilized to create thermal barrier coatings (TBCs) primarily in aerospace and industrial applications. TBCs enhance the thermal resistance of components such as turbine blades and combustion chambers, allowing them to operate at elevated temperatures while improving efficiency and longevity. The EB-PVD method ensures a high-quality and durable coating by using focused electron beams to evaporate material, which then deposits in a controlled manner. </p><p>The Electron Beam Physical Vapor Deposition Thermal Barrier Coatings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period.
