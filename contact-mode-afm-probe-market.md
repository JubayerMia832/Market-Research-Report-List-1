<p><p>Contact mode Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) probes are essential tools in nanoscale imaging and measurement. The market mainly comprises Arrow Tip and Rotating Tip probes. Arrow Tip probes, characterized by their sharp geometry, are optimized for high-resolution imaging, making them suitable for soft and hard materials. In contrast, Rotating Tip probes excel in reducing friction during scanning, facilitating improved accuracy and faster imaging speeds. Both types enhance surface characterization, catering to diverse applications in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
